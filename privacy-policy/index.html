Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 09, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Content you choose to save through the share extension
  • Basic usage statistics to improve the app
  • Optional notification preferences

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate your personal weekly newspaper
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send notifications (if enabled)

Data Storage and Security

All your saved content is stored locally on your device. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect any data that is synchronized.

Third-Party Services

We don't share your data with third parties. When you save content from external sources, we only store the necessary information to display it in your digest.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your saved content
  • Delete any saved content
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of analytics

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@savedpress.com
